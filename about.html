About Us!

Our Mission

Our mission is to build a community of people who have a place to feel comfortable and have fun. We want video gamers to think of 1-Up Café as a relaxing spot where they can meet people and play video games while also consuming good food and drinks.

Our Story

1-Up Café is a video game café based in New York City. The owners, David Mena (left) and Jared Singh (right), opened the café because they always wanted to hang out in a spot that had two of their favorite things, food and video games. They decided to follow this passion and open a location in Manhattan and the rest is history.
